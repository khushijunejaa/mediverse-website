About Mediverse

Mediverse is a healthcare consulting and medical infrastructure advisory firm partnering with hospitals, institutions and government bodies. Our work spans strategic consulting, government healthcare projects, institutional partnerships, procurement advisory and enterprise business growth.

We bring precision to complex healthcare decisions — from ICU and NICU capability planning to radiology deployment and turnkey delivery — grounded in more than fifty years of combined leadership across the industry.
